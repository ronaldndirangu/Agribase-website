About Agribase Consultants

Agribase was established and registered as a consultancy firm in 2009. We have permanent staffs and professional associates coordinated by Godfrey Ndirangu, the director, in terms of quality control of proposals and the reports delivered to the client. We have been contracted to provide consultancy services and supply goods and services in over 15 contracts in the last three years in Kenya and other countries in the East African region.

The firm offers research, consultancy services and technical management assistance in the following broad areas:

  • Community Project Appraisal
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Baseline surveys and impact assessment
  • Conducting feasibility studies and engineering designs for irrigation, water, and other related projects
  • Carrying out Environmental and Social Impact Assessments
  • Policy research and Planning
  • Advisory services and research in agriculture, including marketing and farm management
  • Community based capacity building and organizational development (OD), post-harvest and agro-processing, etc.
